OUR COMPANY

Rosetta Clinic was created by Heather to help men and women achieve their beauty goals without the need for complicated and risky surgical procedures. The beauty clinic uses the latest non-invasive treatments and is accredited by the British Association of Beauty Therapy and Cosmetology (BABTAC) and the Guild of Beauty Therapists to bring you the leading innovative treatments in Preston.

Rosetta specialises in IPL laser treatments which can be used to treat a variety of skin conditions as well as being the best solution for hair removal. Also, Heather is qualified to perform Microneedling and Fibroblast Plasma treatments, which are industry leaders for collagen production and skin tightening.

Our Mission


Heather knows that there is no better feeling than having a flawless skin day as it can really give you an extra confidence boost. Well, imagine having a flawless skin day, every day. How great would that be?

Rosetta's mission is to have every client leave the clinic feeling more confident and happy than when they arrived. With the assistance of advanced training, extensive scientific research, the latest state of the art equipment and luxury Dermalogica products; Rosetta delivers results that go a long way to improving the quality of client's lives and giving them the flawless, younger and more radiant looking skin that they have been looking for.
